## Autonomous Tractors & Field Robots

Robotic systems designed for large-scale field operations such as planting, spraying, and tilling.

- [AGCO Fendt GuideConnect](https://www.fendt.com/) — Autonomous tractor guidance system for coordinated field operations.
- [John Deere Autonomous Tractor](https://www.deere.com/en/autonomous-tractor/) — Self-driving tractor for precision agriculture tasks.
- [Naïo Technologies](https://www.naio-technologies.com/) — Autonomous robots for farming tasks such as hoeing and harvesting.
- [Raven Autonomy](https://ravenind.com/) — Precision agriculture and autonomous solutions for field machinery.

## Crop Monitoring & Sensing

Robotic and sensor-based systems for monitoring crop health, soil conditions, and environmental data.

- [Arable Mark](https://www.arable.com/) — Field monitoring system combining sensors and analytics for crop insights.
- [CropX](https://www.cropx.com/) — Soil sensing and analytics platform for precision irrigation and crop management.
- [Prospera](https://www.prospera.ag/) — AI-powered crop monitoring platform for greenhouse and field agriculture.
- [Taranis](https://taranis.com/) — Aerial imaging and analytics for crop intelligence.

## Harvesting Robots

Robotic systems designed to automate the harvesting of fruits, vegetables, and specialty crops.

- [Abundant Robotics](https://www.abundantrobotics.com/) — Robotic apple harvesting system using vacuum-based picking.
- [Agrobot](https://www.agrobot.com/) — Robotic harvesting systems for strawberries and other crops.
- [FFRobotics](https://ffrobotics.com/) — Autonomous fruit picking robots using computer vision and robotic arms.
- [Octinion Rubion](https://octinion.com/rubion/) — Strawberry picking robot designed for greenhouse environments.

## Robotics Platforms & Frameworks

Software and hardware platforms for building and deploying agricultural robots.

- [ROS (Robot Operating System)](https://www.ros.org/) — Open-source robotics middleware widely used in agricultural robotics.
- [PX4](https://px4.io/) — Open-source flight control software for drones and autonomous systems.
- [NVIDIA Isaac](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac) — Robotics development platform with AI and simulation capabilities.

## Simulation & Research

Tools, datasets, and research initiatives supporting agricultural robotics development.

- [AgML](https://agml.ai/) — Open-source dataset and tools for agricultural machine learning.
- [Farm-ng](https://farm-ng.com/) — Robotics platform and research initiative for small-scale autonomous farming.
- [Open Agriculture Initiative (MIT)](https://www.media.mit.edu/groups/open-agriculture-openag/overview/) — Research project exploring controlled-environment agriculture and automation.

## Weeding & Precision Agriculture

Robotic systems focused on weed control, targeted spraying, and resource-efficient farming.

- [Blue River Technology](https://bluerivertechnology.com/) — Precision spraying systems using computer vision for weed control.
- [Ecorobotix](https://www.ecorobotix.com/) — AI-powered weeding robots for precision agriculture.
- [FarmWise](https://farmwise.io/) — Autonomous weeding robots using machine vision.
- [Naïo Dino](https://www.naio-technologies.com/en/dino/) — Robotic weeder designed for vegetable farming.
